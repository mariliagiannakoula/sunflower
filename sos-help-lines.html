Before calling a help-line

You might want to consider:

  1. What times are they open?
  2. Is it free to call or is there a cost involved?
  3. Is what you say confidential? For example, many services have policies on what to do if someone says they have attempted suicide or are actively planning to.
  4. What will you do if the line is busy? It's often worth trying several times, or you might plan to call back later or try a different service.
